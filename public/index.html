About IntelliumX

Built by an engineer. Designed for real people.

Modern technology offers incredible possibilities—but too often it’s difficult to understand or more complicated than it needs to be.

IntelliumX builds practical cloud and AI solutions that make powerful technology easier to use through simplicity, security, and thoughtful design.

Every product begins with one question: How can we make this easier for people?

Practical Solutions

Technology should solve real problems—not create new ones.

Simple by Design

Built on Trust

Security, reliability, and continuous improvement are built into everything we create.

Meet the Founder

IntelliumX was founded by Nobuhiro Tokushige, a software engineer with over 20 years of experience designing, supporting, and delivering enterprise software solutions. Drawing on expertise in cloud computing, AI, and automation, he founded IntelliumX to build practical technology that helps people solve real-world problems through simple, reliable, and secure software.
