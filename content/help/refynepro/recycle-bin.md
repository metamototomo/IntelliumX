## Deleting a Document

Right-click any document and choose **Delete**. The document moves to the Recycle Bin — it is not permanently removed yet.

## Restoring a Document

1. Click the **Recycle Bin** tab in the File Manager.
2. Right-click the document and choose **Restore**.

## Permanently Deleting

In the Recycle Bin, right-click a document and choose **Delete Permanently**. This action cannot be undone.
