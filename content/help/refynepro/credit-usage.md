## Viewing Your Usage

Open the **Settings** panel and select the **Usage** tab. You will see:

- Tokens used this month
- Remaining balance
- Per-action breakdown

## Monthly Reset

Credits reset automatically at the start of each billing cycle.

## Setting a Usage Limit

In the **Usage** tab, enter a monthly token limit to prevent unexpected overages. Refyne Pro will alert you when you are approaching the limit.
