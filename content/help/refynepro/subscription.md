## View Your Plan

Open the **Account** panel and select **Subscription** to see your current plan, renewal date, and included credits.

## Upgrade or Change Plan

Click **Change Plan** to view available options and select a new tier. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

## Cancel Subscription

To cancel, click **Cancel Subscription** at the bottom of the Subscription page. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
