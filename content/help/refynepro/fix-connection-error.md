## Symptoms

- "Unable to connect" error on startup
- Requests time out after a few seconds

## Steps to Resolve

1. Check your internet connection.
2. Verify that your firewall is not blocking Refyne Pro.
3. Navigate to **Settings → Network** and confirm the server URL is correct.
4. Click **Test Connection** to validate the settings.

## Still Not Working?

Contact support at support@refynepro.com with your connection log attached.
