## Enable MFA

1. Open the **Account** panel from the top-right menu.
2. Go to **Security → Multi-Factor Authentication**.
3. Click **Enable MFA** and scan the QR code with your authenticator app.
4. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm setup.

MFA is required at every sign-in once enabled.
