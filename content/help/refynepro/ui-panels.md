### File Manager:

You can manage your documents using folder structure. There are additional functions to help you easily manage your documents as you work.

### Main Area:

This is the text area where you primarily work and create valuable information.

### Settings Panel:

Select an AI model and configure AI behaviours.

### Account Panel:

Manage your user account. You can open and close these panels by clicking the corresponding buttons.
