## Installation

Download the latest version of Refyne Pro from the official website and run the installer.

## First Launch

On first launch, you will be prompted to sign in with your account. Enter your credentials and click **Sign In**.

## Next Steps

Once signed in, explore the dashboard to familiarise yourself with the main features.
