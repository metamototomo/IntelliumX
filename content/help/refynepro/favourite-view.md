## Pinning a Document

Right-click any document and choose **Add to Favourites**, or press **Cmd+K** and select **Pin**.

## Accessing Favourites

Click the **Favourites** tab in the File Manager panel to see all pinned documents in one place.

## Removing a Favourite

Right-click the document in Favourites view and choose **Remove from Favourites**.
