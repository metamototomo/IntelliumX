## Accessing AI Settings

Navigate to **Settings → AI Model** from the main menu.

## Available Options

- **Model**: Select the AI model that best suits your use case.
- **Temperature**: Controls response creativity. Lower values produce more predictable output.
- **Max Tokens**: Sets the maximum length of AI responses.

## Saving Changes

Click **Save** after adjusting settings. Changes take effect immediately.
