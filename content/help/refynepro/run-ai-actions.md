## Running an AI Action

1. Select the text you want to improve in the editor.
2. Open the context menu with **Cmd+K** or right-click.
3. Choose an action — **Improve**, **Summarise**, **Simplify**, **Professional**, or **Casual**.
4. The result appears in the **Result panel** on the right.

## Applying the Result

- Click **Apply** to replace your selected text with the AI output.
- Click **Discard** to dismiss the result and keep your original text.

## Custom Prompts

For full control, choose **Custom Prompt** and type your own instruction.
